I am a PhD student in Philosophy at the University of Oslo (UiO).
My research is in logic and philosophy of mathematics. I’m particularly interested in the philosophy of modal logic and how it can be used in foundations of mathematics.

My PhD project is supervised by Prof. Øystein Linnebo and is funded by the ERC Advanced Project Construction in Formal Sciences (C-FORS). 

I obtained my master's degree from the University of the Italian Switzerland in 2024 and my bachelor's degree from the Catholic University of Milan in 2022. 

This website collects:
- my **CV**,
- short **blog posts and news**,
- and my **papers and drafts**.
